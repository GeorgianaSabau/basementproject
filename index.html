5 SECONDS GAME!

The youngest player is first and the play will move to the left. The player to the right reads the question aloud.

The player that reads the question should then start the timer or count until 5. You have 5 seconds to give three answers.

If you say three answers before the timer is off or the count is of, you get 1 point.
If another player deems an answer given to be questionable, the group as a whole should decide if the answer is acceptable.

If you are able to name only one of two answers before 5 seconds are up, you will get no points and the play moves to the left.

The next player then gets 5 seconds to try to answer the same question but cannot use any answers that were previously used for that card.

NEVER HAVE I EVER!

The game is started with the players getting into a circle.

Then, the first player read aloud a simple statement.
